Social Engineering

The Art of Human Hacking

person_search What is it & Why it Matters

A psychological manipulation technique used to trick individuals into revealing confidential information or performing actions that compromise security. It exploits human emotions and cognitive biases, making it one of the most effective methods for cybercriminals to bypass technical defenses.

It exploits human psychology for fraud, data theft, and unauthorized system access. Humans are easier to exploit than technical systems.

It matters because it bypasses encryptions targeting the unpatchable factor: human trust. Exploiting human natural curiosity & a desire to help, attackers turn users into "keys" to access systems.

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a deceptive technique that uses digital communications—email, text, or social media—to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information or performing actions that compromise security.

Active Defense Strategies

Spotting inconsistencies before interacting.

mail

Phishing Emails

  • • Identity: Look beyond the name; check actual email domain.
  • • Tone: Watch for "Act Now" or fake urgency.
  • • Links: Hover before clicking. Does it match the company?
language

Websites

  • • URL: Look for extra words (e.g., secure-bank-login.com).
  • • HTTPS: The lock icon only means encrypted, not safe.
  • • Context: Did you type the URL or follow a link?
sms

Smishing (SMS)

  • • Sender: Random numbers or slightly altered names.
  • • Links: Avoid shortened URLs (bit.ly, tinyurl).
  • • Requests: Banks rarely ask for OTPs via text.
call

Vishing (Voice)

  • • Behavior: High pressure and rushing you.
  • • Secrets: Never share PINs or OTPs over a call.
  • • Verify: Hang up and call the official bank number.

Legal & Security Rights

Empowering You with Legal Knowledge

public International Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

The GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (USA) set the global gold standard. They grant you the "Right to be Forgotten" and mandate that companies must notify you of a data breach within 72 hours. These laws ensure your data is treated as your personal property.

flag Ghana Data Protection Act (Act 843)

Ghana's Act 843 and the Cybersecurity Act (Act 1038) provide a local safety net. It is illegal for institutions to leak your MoMo details or personal data. You have the legal right to demand how your data is used and to hold negligent organizations accountable.

person_shield For You (Individual)

  • ✔ Reporting is a Right: You are protected when reporting fraud to the CSA via 292.
  • ✔ Identity Protection: Victims of "Sakawa" or Sextortion have the right to privacy and legal support.

corporate_fare For Business (GRC)

  • ✔ Governance & Compliance: Companies must appoint a Data Protection Officer (DPO) under Ghana law.
  • ✔ Liability: Organizations are legally liable for financial losses caused by poor employee phishing training.
